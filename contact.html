FAQs

Do I need prior modeling experience?

Not at all. We welcome complete newcomers. What matters most is confidence, drive, and the right look for our clients.

What age range do you accept?

We work with models aged 16 and above, across all fashion categories including editorial, commercial, and runway.

Is there a fee to apply?

Absolutely not. Submitting is always free. We earn our commission only when you earn — that’s our promise.

How long does the review process take?

We aim to respond within 48 business hours. During fashion weeks it may take up to 5 days.
